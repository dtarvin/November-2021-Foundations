Steps for Making a PB & J

  1. Gather needed supplies:
    • Peanut Butter
    • Jelly
    • Bread
    • Knife
  2. Take out two slices of bread
  3. Put peanut butter on one side of one slice
  4. Put Jelly on one side of the other slice
  5. Put both slices together so that the peanut butter and jelly are together
  6. Eat to your heart's content!
  7. Important Notes:
    • Always have a plate under you
    • Always use a napkin/paper towel to keep food off your face
